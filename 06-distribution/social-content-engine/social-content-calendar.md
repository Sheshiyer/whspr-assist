# WHSPR Assist -- 30-Day Pre-Launch Social Content Calendar

**Brand:** WHSPR Assist | **Product:** WHSPR Assist Rx
**Tagline:** Hear more. See more. Live more.
**Voice:** Dr. Maren (B2C social -- warm, conversational, Grade 6-7 reading level)
**Calendar Period:** 30 days pre-launch (T-30 to Launch Day)
**Content Mix:** 40% Value/Education | 30% Connection/BTS | 20% Engagement/Polls | 10% Promotion/CTA

---

## Weekly Theme Overview

| Week | Theme | Objective | Emotional Driver |
|------|-------|-----------|-----------------|
| **1** (Days 1-7) | Pain Awareness | Surface relatable moments people brush off | Recognition, validation |
| **2** (Days 8-14) | Solution Education | Introduce WHSPR Assist as a category, not a product | Curiosity, hope |
| **3** (Days 15-21) | Social Proof and Trust | Build credibility through stories, clinical backing, real moments | Trust, confidence |
| **4** (Days 22-30) | Launch Hype | Drive waitlist signups and anticipation | Excitement, urgency |

---

## Content Mix Guide

- **Value/Education (40%):** Tips, misconceptions, "did you know" facts, explainers
- **Connection/BTS (30%):** Founder stories, design process, team moments, Dr. Maren perspective
- **Engagement/Polls (20%):** Questions, polls, "this or that," comment prompts
- **Promotion/CTA (10%):** Waitlist, launch countdown, direct asks

---

## Platform Targeting Key

- **IG** = Instagram (Reels, Carousels, Stories)
- **TT** = TikTok (Short-form video)
- **TW** = Twitter/X (Threads, single tweets)
- **LI** = LinkedIn (Thought leadership, professional)
- **FB** = Facebook (Community, shared content)

## Notes for Content Team

1. **Voice Consistency:** All captions are written in Dr. Maren voice -- warm, direct, Grade 6-7 reading level. Adapt for platform but preserve the register.
2. **Media Production:** Descriptions are creative briefs for the production team. Prioritize authenticity over polish. Real people, warm light, genuine moments.
3. **Prohibited Language:** Never use "hearing loss," "disability," "impairment," "elderly," "senior," "old," "device," "fix," "cure," "treat." See brand guidelines for full list.
4. **Engagement Strategy:** Respond to every comment in the first hour. Use the same warm, direct voice. Never be clinical in replies.
5. **Cross-posting:** Adapt format per platform. Reels for IG/TT, threads for TW, articles for LI. Same message, native format.
6. **Accessibility:** All video content needs captions. All images need alt text. This is a hearing-enhancement brand -- lead by example.
7. **Waitlist Tracking:** Every CTA post should use unique UTM parameters per platform for attribution tracking.
