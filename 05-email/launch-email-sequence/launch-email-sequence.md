**How hearing enhancement works in your glasses:**

Every pair of WHSPR Assist Rx contains a dual-microphone system with ANC/ENC (active noise cancellation and environmental noise control). Here is what that means in plain language:

**Two microphones. One job.**
The microphones sit in the temples of the frame, positioned to pick up sound from the direction you are facing. One captures the voice in front of you. The other maps the noise around you -- clinking glasses, background music, kitchen sounds, other conversations.

**The system separates voice from noise.**
In real time, the processor filters out ambient noise and amplifies the voice you are facing. The result: the person across the table comes through clearly, while the restaurant hum fades back.

**Open-ear speakers deliver sound naturally.**
The speakers sit near your ears but do not go inside them. You hear the enhanced audio layered on top of the natural sound around you -- not replacing it. You are still in the room. You are just hearing more of what matters.
