**Trigger:** Immediately upon VIP list signup
**From:** Dr. Maren at WHSPR Assist (maren@whspr.com)

|---------|-------------|

- Early access before the public launch
- $249 locked-in pricing (up to $100 off standard)
- First pick of frame styles when pre-orders open
- Priority shipping when production begins
- Direct updates from our team — real information, not marketing blasts

**Origin Story:**
> WHSPR Assist Rx started with a question I hear in my practice every week: *Why is there nothing that helps me hear more clearly AND fits my prescription — without costing three thousand dollars?*

## Pre-Launch Sequence

### Email PL-1: T-7 Announcement
**Timing:** 7 days before launch (Tuesday/Wednesday 9:00 AM EST)
**Expected Open Rate:** 45–55%
**Expected Click Rate:** 12–18%

#### Subject Lines by Segment

| Variant | Target | Subject Line |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| A | George | One week from today, you get first look at WHSPR Assist Rx |
| B | George | {{FIRST_NAME}}, your VIP preview of WHSPR Assist Rx is ready |
| C | Sarah | The gift that gives Dad back his favorite conversations — preview inside |

#### George Version — Key Blocks
- Acknowledgment of why they signed up (hearing + seeing clearly)
- One-week countdown announcement
- Three VIP benefits: early page access, first-in-line pricing, direct team line
- Pricing tier breakdown ($249–$499)
- Timeline: today (preview) → T-1 (VIP link) → launch day (VIP window)

#### Sarah Version — Key Blocks
- Acknowledgment of caregiver concern (someone missing conversations)
- Behavioral observations (TV volume, nodding along)
- Product as something he would actually wear
- Caregiver Bundle highlight ($499 for two pairs)
- Family share CTA
