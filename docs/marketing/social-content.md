### Category 1: Negative Warning Hooks

#### NW-01 — Stop Buying Hearing Aids Nobody Wears
**Audience:** Sarah (35–55, caregiver)
**Platforms:** TikTok, Instagram Reels

**Pattern Interrupt:** Kitchen junk drawer with dusty hearing aid. Hand closes drawer. Cut to WHSPR Assist on warm bedside table.

**Voiceover:**
> "Stop spending thirty-two hundred dollars on something that stays in the drawer. These are prescription smart glasses with hearing enhancement built in. $299. And he actually wears them."

- 0:04–0:08 → "WHSPR Assist Rx — $299"
- 0:09–0:12 → "Hearing enhancement + Rx lenses"
- 0:13–0:15 → "He actually wears them." | CTA

## Influencer Outreach Strategy

### Target Niches

| Niche | Platform Skew | Content Themes |
|-------|---------------|----------------|
| Active Aging / Living Well After 50 | Instagram, YouTube | Fitness, retirement, grandparenting, style |
| Caregiver Communities | TikTok, Instagram | Parent care, aging communication, gift ideas |
| Audiology & Optometry Professionals | Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn | Hearing education, product reviews, myth-busting |
| Assistive Tech & Accessibility | YouTube, TikTok | Smart glasses reviews, wearable roundups |
| AARP-Adjacent Lifestyle | Facebook, Instagram | Wellness routines, quality-of-life upgrades |

### Influencer Tiers

| Tier | Followers | Volume | Budget |
|------|-----------|--------|--------|
| Micro | 10K–50K | 30–50 creators | 40% |
| Mid | 50K–200K | 10–20 creators | 35% |
| Macro | 200K+ | 3–8 creators | 25% |

### Value Proposition for Creators

- Free product (one pair, $249–$349 value)
- Early access (2–4 weeks before public)
- 10% affiliate commission on all sales through unique link
- Behind-the-scenes content and founder interviews
- **Full creative freedom** — no scripts, no talking points, no editorial control

### Outreach Rules

- Three total touches maximum per creator
- Each follow-up must add new value
- Archive if no response after step 3; revisit in 90 days
- Never use urgency or scarcity language
