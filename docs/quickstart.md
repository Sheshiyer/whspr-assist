## What Is WHSPR Assist Rx?

WHSPR Assist Rx is the **first prescription-ready smart glasses** that combine hearing enhancement, AI-powered vision assistance, and everyday eyewear in a single frame — for under $350.

It was built for the **80% of Americans with hearing loss who refuse traditional hearing aids** — because those options cost too much, look too medical, and require too much compromise.

## The Brand Voice

WHSPR Assist speaks as **Dr. Maren** — a 52-year-old optometrist who talks like a trusted clinician you have known for years. Direct, warm, free of jargon, never condescending.

**Core principles:**
- **Dignity first** — every word must pass the test: would George feel respected reading this?
- **Enhancement framing** — we make things better, we don't fix what's broken
- **Never use** — "hearing loss," "elderly," "disability," "suffer from," "easy," "revolutionary"
- **Always use** — "hear more clearly," "smart glasses," "hearing enhancement," "clarity," "connection"

## Competitive Position

WHSPR Assist creates a **new category below hearing aids** — where the 80% of untreated hearing loss lives. It does not compete with Phonak or Oticon on audiological performance. It competes against **doing nothing**.

| Competitor | Price | What's Missing |
|-----------|-------|----------------|
| Phonak Audeo Infinio | $2,498–$4,398 | Stigma, no vision, 10x price |
| Starkey Omega AI | $4,500–$6,500 | 18x price, feature overwhelm |
| OrCam MyEye 2.0 | $4,500 | No hearing, clip-on, 13x price |
| Ray-Ban Meta | $379–$799 | No Rx lenses, no hearing enhancement |
| **WHSPR Assist Rx** | **$249–$349** | **Category of one** |
