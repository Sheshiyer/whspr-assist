# B2B Whitelabel Partner Program

> *Hear more. See more. Live more.*

The WHSPR Assist Partner Program enables audiology practices, optometry clinics, hearing health networks, and optical retail chains to offer WHSPR Assist smart glasses as part of their clinical product portfolio.

Partners purchase devices at wholesale pricing, apply their preferred level of branding customization, and deliver the product to patients through their existing clinical workflow.

## Tier Details

### Silver — Entry Point

**For:** Independent practices and small groups evaluating the smart glasses category.

**Included capabilities:**
- Standard WHSPR Assist product in all frame styles (V03-T5, V03-T6, V03-T8, V06-T)
- Co-branded packaging insert with partner logo and contact information
- Clinic portal access for order management, patient registration, and device tracking
- Listing in the WHSPR Assist "Find a Provider" directory (app and web)
- Standard product training materials (digital)
- Email and phone support with 48-hour response SLA

**Not included:** Custom frame colors, temple engraving, app branding, custom wake word, dedicated account management.

### Gold — Regional Differentiation

**For:** Regional clinic groups (3–15 locations) wanting visible brand differentiation.

**Additional capabilities (beyond Silver):**
- Custom temple arm colors from extended palette (12 colorways)
- Temple engraving with partner logo (up to 40 characters)
- Co-branded outer packaging with partner logo placement
- Branded companion app splash screen and home screen logo
- Custom color accent throughout the app interface
- Branded onboarding sequence for new patients
- Priority support with 24-hour response SLA
- Quarterly business review with partner success team
- Staff training webinar (live, 4 hours, up to 20 attendees)

### Platinum — Full Brand Ownership

**For:** National chains, hospital systems, VA networks, and franchise organizations.

**Additional capabilities (beyond Gold):**
- Full Pantone color matching for temple arms (any color)
- Custom wake word (replaces "Hey Cyan")
- Fully custom packaging (partner-designed box, inserts, pouch)
- Fully skinned companion app published under partner's App Store account
- Custom app name, icon, and color scheme
- Custom clinic locator showing only partner locations
- Removal of WHSPR Assist branding from all patient-facing UI
- Exclusive territory agreements (geographic or channel-based)
- Dedicated account manager with 4-hour response SLA
- On-site staff training (up to 3 locations per year)
- Co-developed marketing materials and campaign support
- Early access to new hardware and firmware releases

## Onboarding Process

### Silver (2 weeks)
1. Partner agreement signed
2. Initial order placed (minimum 5 units)
3. Portal access provisioned
4. Digital training materials delivered
5. Co-branded insert artwork submitted and approved
6. First shipment dispatched

### Gold (4 weeks)
1. Partner agreement + branding brief submitted
2. Custom color and engraving selections confirmed
3. App branding assets submitted (logo, accent color)
4. Staff training webinar scheduled
5. Co-branded packaging proofs approved
6. Initial order shipped

### Platinum (8 weeks)
1. Partner agreement + full brand kit submitted
2. Dedicated account manager assigned
3. Custom wake word defined and tested
4. Fully skinned app submitted for App Store review
5. Custom packaging manufactured and approved
6. On-site training scheduled
7. Launch support plan finalized
8. Initial inventory shipped
