# WHSPR Assist Product Skinning Guide

## 1. Overview

This document defines the physical product customization options available to WHSPR Assist whitelabel partners. Customization scope increases with partner tier (Silver, Gold, Platinum) and covers frame modifications, lens configurations, packaging co-branding, and fulfillment logistics.

All customizations are applied at the factory level during production. Partners submit customization specifications during onboarding, and all artwork and color selections require written approval before production begins. For companion app customization, refer to the [App Skinning Guide](app-skinning-guide.md).

---

## 2. Frame Customization

### 2.1 Available Frame Models

All partners have access to the full WHSPR Assist frame lineup:

| Model | Style | Description | Target Patient |
|-------|-------|-------------|---------------|
| V03-T5 | Square | Classic rectangular frame | Broad face shapes, professional settings |
| V03-T6 | Round | Rounded frame profile | Smaller face shapes, casual settings |
| V03-T8 | Semi-Rimless | Minimalist lower-rimless design | Patients preferring lightweight, discreet appearance |
| V06-T | Sport Square | Wrap-around sport frame | Active patients, outdoor use |

### 2.2 Temple Arm Color Options

Temple arm color customization is the primary visual differentiator for partner-branded units.

| Tier | Color Options | Process |
|------|--------------|---------|
| Silver | Standard palette (8 colors) | Select from stock; no setup fee |
| Gold | Extended palette (20 colors) | Select from extended catalog; no setup fee |
| Platinum | Full Pantone matching | Submit Pantone reference; $500 one-time setup; color proof required |

**Standard Palette (All Tiers):**
Matte Black, Gloss Black, Graphite, Navy, Tortoise Shell, Brushed Silver, Warm Bronze, Classic Brown

**Extended Palette (Gold and Platinum):**
All standard colors plus: Forest Green, Burgundy, Ocean Blue, Slate Grey, Rose Gold, Champagne, Pearl White, Charcoal, Espresso, Copper, Sage, Dusty Blue

**Pantone Matching (Platinum Only):**
Partners submit a Pantone Solid Coated reference number. A physical color proof is produced and shipped for approval before production begins. Proofing adds 5 -- 7 business days to the timeline. Color match tolerance is Delta E less than 2.0.

### 2.3 Temple Engraving

| Tier | Engraving Options |
|------|------------------|
| Silver | Not available |
| Gold | Partner logo (vector artwork required, max 15mm x 5mm) |
| Platinum | Partner logo + custom text (up to 40 characters total, max 40mm x 5mm) |

**Artwork Requirements:**
- Format: SVG or AI (vector only; no rasterized artwork)
- Minimum line weight: 0.3mm
- Engraving method: Laser etching on temple arm interior surface
- Color: Single-color (engraving depth creates contrast against temple arm color)
- Proof: Digital proof provided within 3 business days of artwork submission

### 2.4 Frame Material

All frames use TR (Thermoplastic Rubber) construction. Frame material is not customizable per partner; the material specification is standardized across all production for quality consistency and durability compliance.

## 3. Lens Options

All tiers have access to the full lens catalog. Lens selection is made per-unit at time of order, allowing partners to stock a mix of configurations.

| Lens Type | Description | Added Cost (over base) | All Tiers |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|-----------|
| Clear | Standard optical-grade polycarbonate | Included | Yes |
| Anti-Blue Light | Blue light filtering for screen use | +$8/unit | Yes |
| Photochromic | Auto-darkening in sunlight | +$15/unit | Yes |
| Tinted (non-polarized) | Fixed tint for sun protection | +$5/unit | Yes |
| Polarized | Glare reduction for outdoor use | +$12/unit | Yes |
| Prescription-ready (uncut) | Ships without lenses; partner optical lab cuts to prescription | -$3/unit (lens credit) | Yes |

**Prescription Workflow:**
Partners who operate their own optical labs may order frames without lenses (prescription-ready configuration). The frame accepts standard optical lab lens cutting equipment. WHSPR Assist provides lens template files (DXF format) for each frame model upon request.

## 5. Packaging Customization

### 5.1 Box Exterior

| Tier | Box Exterior Options |
|------|---------------------|
| Silver | Standard WHSPR Assist box (no modification) |
| Gold | Co-branded box -- partner logo on front panel, WHSPR Assist logo on side panel |
| Platinum | Fully custom box -- partner-designed artwork on all surfaces; WHSPR Assist attribution on bottom panel only |

**Gold Box Specifications:**
- Partner logo placement: Front panel, lower-right quadrant
- Maximum logo dimensions: 35mm x 15mm
- Logo format: CMYK vector (AI or PDF)
- Box base design: WHSPR Assist standard (Trust Navy with Warm Linen accents)

**Platinum Box Specifications:**
- Full bleed custom artwork on all 6 panels
- Minimum requirement: Product name and partner branding on front panel; WHSPR Assist manufacturing attribution on bottom panel (regulatory requirement)
- Artwork format: CMYK PDF, 300 DPI minimum, with 3mm bleed
- Structural proofing: Physical box prototype shipped for approval before production

### 5.2 Interior Insert Card

| Tier | Insert Card Options |
|------|---------------------|
| Silver | Co-branded insert -- partner logo, clinic address, appointment phone number |
| Gold | Custom-designed insert -- partner branding, content approved by WHSPR Assist |
| Platinum | Fully custom insert -- partner-designed, no WHSPR Assist branding required |

Insert cards are printed on 300gsm matte cardstock, 100mm x 150mm. Partners supply artwork in PDF format with 3mm bleed.

### 5.3 Carrying Pouch

| Tier | Pouch Options |
|------|--------------|
| Silver | Standard WHSPR Assist pouch (microfiber, embossed logo) |
| Gold | Co-branded pouch -- partner logo debossed alongside WHSPR Assist logo |
| Platinum | Custom pouch -- partner logo only, choice of microfiber or vegan leather |

**Debossing Specifications:**
- Maximum logo area: 30mm x 12mm
- Artwork: Single-color vector (debossing is monochrome)
- Pouch color options: Charcoal, Navy, Stone (standard); custom color available for Platinum (+$2/unit)

## 7. Quality Assurance

All customized units undergo the same quality assurance process as standard WHSPR Assist production:

- Housing seal integrity testing (100% of units)
- Battery cycle validation (sample-based, 5% of batch)
- Bluetooth 5.4 pairing verification (100% of units)
- Camera and audio functional test (100% of units)
- Cosmetic inspection including engraving quality (100% of customized units)
- Color match verification against approved proof (first 10 units of each Pantone order)

Defect rate target: Less than 1% of shipped units. Partners report defects through the clinic portal; replacement units ship per the SLA defined in the [Partner Program Overview](../partner-program-overview/partner-program-overview.md).

## 8. Artwork Submission Checklist

Before production can begin on customized orders, partners must submit and receive approval on all applicable artwork:

| Asset | Format | Required For |
|-------|--------|-------------|
| Partner logo (vector) | SVG or AI | Gold, Platinum |
| Partner logo (CMYK print) | AI or PDF | Gold, Platinum |
| Temple engraving artwork | SVG or AI, max 40mm x 5mm | Gold, Platinum |
| Box exterior artwork | CMYK PDF, 300 DPI, 3mm bleed | Gold (co-brand), Platinum (full custom) |
| Insert card artwork | CMYK PDF, 300 DPI, 3mm bleed | All tiers |
| Pouch debossing artwork | Single-color vector | Gold, Platinum |
| Pantone reference number | Pantone Solid Coated | Platinum |

Submit all artwork to the dedicated partner portal or to your account manager. Artwork review and proof turnaround is 3 business days from submission.
