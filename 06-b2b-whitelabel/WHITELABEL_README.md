## The 3-Tier Partner Model

WHSPR Assist operates a tiered partner program designed to scale from independent clinics to national retail chains. Each tier unlocks progressively deeper customization, better economics, and dedicated support.

### Tier Summary

| Attribute | Silver | Gold | Platinum |
|-----------|--------|------|----------|
| **Quarterly Volume** | 5 -- 24 units | 25 -- 99 units | 100+ units |
| **Wholesale Price** | $140/unit | $130/unit | $120/unit |
| **Frame Customization** | Standard palette only | Custom temple colors | Full Pantone matching |
| **Temple Engraving** | Not available | Partner logo | Partner logo + custom text |
| **Packaging** | Co-branded insert | Co-branded box + insert | Fully custom packaging |
| **App Experience** | Standard app, clinic listed in directory | Branded splash + home screen | Fully skinned app under partner name |
| **Wake Word** | "Hey Cyan" (standard) | "Hey Cyan" (standard) | Custom wake word available |
| **Support** | Standard (48h response) | Priority (24h response) | Dedicated account manager (4h response) |
| **Territory** | Non-exclusive | Non-exclusive | Exclusive territory available |
| **Onboarding Timeline** | 2 weeks | 4 weeks | 8 weeks |

### Choosing the Right Tier

- **Silver** is designed for independent audiology practices and small optical shops testing the category. Low commitment, standard product, meaningful margin improvement over retail.
- **Gold** suits regional clinic groups (3-15 locations) that want visible brand differentiation without managing a separate app distribution. The branded splash screen and custom frame colors create a distinct patient experience.
- **Platinum** is built for national chains, hospital systems, and franchise networks that require complete brand control. The fully skinned app publishes under the partner's App Store account, and patients interact with the partner's brand throughout the experience.

## How to Use This Package

**For Business Development:**
Start with the [Partner Program Overview](partner-program-overview/) to understand tier economics and qualification criteria. Use the tier comparison table in sales conversations.

**For Product and Engineering:**
Review the [Product Skinning Guide](product-app-skinning/product-skinning-guide.md) and [App Skinning Guide](product-app-skinning/app-skinning-guide.md) to understand customization scope per tier. **For Partner Marketing Teams:**
The [Co-Branding Guidelines](co-branding-guidelines/) define permitted logo usage, naming conventions, and photography standards for all partner-created materials.
