# WHSPR Assist Partner Program Overview

## 1. Program Introduction

The WHSPR Assist Partner Program enables audiology practices, optometry clinics, hearing health networks, and optical retail chains to offer WHSPR Assist smart glasses as part of their clinical product portfolio. Partners purchase devices at wholesale pricing, apply their preferred level of branding customization, and deliver the product to patients through their existing clinical workflow.

The program is structured around three tiers -- Silver, Gold, and Platinum -- each calibrated to match different business scales, branding requirements, and support needs. Partners graduate between tiers based on quarterly purchase volume and can negotiate tier placement during initial onboarding for committed volume agreements.

This document covers tier structure, pricing, revenue modeling, onboarding processes, and service level agreements. For product customization details, refer to the [Product Skinning Guide](../product-app-skinning/product-skinning-guide.md) and [App Skinning Guide](../product-app-skinning/app-skinning-guide.md).

## 2. Tier Structure

### 2.1 Silver Tier

**Volume Requirement:** 5 -- 24 units per quarter

Silver is the entry point for independent practices and small groups evaluating the smart glasses category. Partners receive co-branded packaging, access to the WHSPR Assist clinic portal, and standard wholesale pricing. The product ships in WHSPR Assist packaging with a co-branded insert card featuring the partner's logo and clinic information.

**Included capabilities:**

- Standard WHSPR Assist product in all available frame styles (V03-T5, V03-T6, V03-T8, V06-T)
- Co-branded packaging insert with partner logo and contact information
- Clinic portal access for order management, patient registration, and device tracking
- Listing in the WHSPR Assist "Find a Provider" directory (app and web)
- Standard product training materials (digital)
- Email and phone support with 48-hour response SLA

**Not included:** Custom frame colors, temple engraving, app branding, custom wake word, dedicated account management.

### 2.2 Gold Tier

**Volume Requirement:** 25 -- 99 units per quarter

Gold is designed for regional clinic groups, mid-size audiology networks, and growing optical chains that want visible differentiation. Partners gain access to custom frame colors from the extended palette, temple engraving with their logo, branded app experiences, and priority support.

**Included capabilities (in addition to Silver):**

- Custom temple arm colors from the extended color palette (12 additional colorways)
- Temple engraving with partner logo (up to 40 characters)
- Co-branded outer packaging with partner logo placement
- Branded companion app splash screen and home screen logo
- Custom color accent throughout the app interface
- Branded onboarding sequence for new patients
- Priority support with 24-hour response SLA
- Quarterly business review with partner success team
- Staff training webinar (live, 4 hours, up to 20 attendees)

**Not included:** Full app skinning, custom wake word, exclusive territory, dedicated account manager, Pantone color matching.

### 2.3 Platinum Tier

**Volume Requirement:** 100+ units per quarter

Platinum is the enterprise tier for national chains, hospital systems, VA networks, and franchise organizations that require complete brand ownership of the patient experience. The product and app operate entirely under the partner's brand, with WHSPR Assist attribution limited to regulatory fine print.

**Included capabilities (in addition to Gold):**

- Full Pantone color matching for temple arms (any color)
- Custom wake word (replaces "Hey Cyan" with partner-defined phrase)
- Fully custom packaging (partner-designed box, inserts, and pouch)
- Fully skinned companion app published under partner's App Store account
- Custom app name, icon, and color scheme
- Custom clinic locator showing only partner locations
- Removal of WHSPR Assist branding from all patient-facing UI
- Exclusive territory agreements (geographic or channel-based)
- Dedicated account manager with 4-hour response SLA
- On-site staff training (up to 3 locations per year)
- Co-developed marketing materials and campaign support
- Early access to new hardware and firmware releases

## 3. Pricing Model

### 3.1 Wholesale Pricing

| Tier | Price Per Unit | Quarterly Minimum | Quarterly Spend (Minimum) |
|------|---------------|-------------------|--------------------------|
| Silver | $140 | 5 units | $700 |
| Gold | $130 | 25 units | $3,250 |
| Platinum | $120 | 100 units | $12,000 |

Pricing is per-unit FOB shipping point. Freight, import duties (if applicable), and insurance are the responsibility of the partner. Volume commitments are evaluated quarterly; partners falling below tier minimums for two consecutive quarters may be reclassified.

### 3.2 Suggested Retail Pricing

Partners set their own retail pricing. WHSPR Assist provides the following guidance to protect brand positioning and ensure adequate margin for clinical fitting services:

| Configuration | Suggested Retail Range |
|---------------|----------------------|
| Standard lens (clear or tinted) | $249 -- $279 |
| Prescription lens (single vision) | $279 -- $319 |
| Prescription lens (progressive) | $319 -- $349 |
| Premium lens (photochromic or polarized) | $299 -- $349 |

### 3.3 Revenue Model

Partners generate revenue through three channels:

1. **Hardware Margin:** Difference between wholesale cost and retail price. At Silver tier ($140 cost, $249 minimum suggested retail), gross margin per unit is approximately $109 (43.8%). At Platinum tier ($120 cost), gross margin increases to approximately $129 (51.8%).

2. **App Subscription Revenue Share:** WHSPR Assist offers an optional premium app tier for patients ($4.99/month) that includes advanced hearing analytics, family sharing for up to 5 devices, and cloud audiogram history. Partners receive 30% of subscription revenue generated by their patients. For Gold and Platinum partners, this share increases to 40% and 50% respectively.

3. **Clinical Fitting Fee:** Partners are encouraged to charge a professional fitting fee ($50 -- $100 recommended) covering hearing profile calibration, frame fitting, lens measurement, and initial training. This fee is retained entirely by the partner and is not shared with WHSPR Assist.

## 5. Service Level Agreements

### 5.1 Support Response Times

| Priority | Silver | Gold | Platinum |
|----------|--------|------|----------|
| Critical (device safety, data breach) | 24 hours | 12 hours | 4 hours |
| High (device malfunction, app outage) | 48 hours | 24 hours | 8 hours |
| Medium (order issue, portal bug) | 72 hours | 48 hours | 24 hours |
| Low (feature request, documentation) | 5 business days | 3 business days | 2 business days |

### 5.2 Platform Uptime

- Clinic portal: 99.5% uptime (all tiers)
- Companion app backend: 99.9% uptime (all tiers)
- API endpoints: 99.9% uptime (Gold and Platinum)
- Dedicated infrastructure: 99.95% uptime (Platinum only)

### 5.3 Device Replacement Policy

| Scenario | Silver | Gold | Platinum |
|----------|--------|------|----------|
| DOA (dead on arrival) | Replacement shipped within 5 business days | 3 business days | Next business day |
| Warranty defect (within 12 months) | Replacement shipped within 7 business days | 5 business days | 3 business days |
| Advanced replacement (swap before return) | Not available | Available ($50 deposit) | Available (no deposit) |
| Extended warranty (months 13 -- 24) | Available for purchase ($25/unit) | Included | Included |

### 5.4 Firmware and App Updates

All partners receive firmware and app updates through the standard release cycle (monthly minor releases, quarterly feature releases). Platinum partners receive early access to beta releases (2 weeks prior to general availability) and can participate in the partner advisory board for feature prioritization.

## 6. Partner Qualification Criteria

Prospective partners must meet the following minimum requirements:

- Active audiology, optometry, or hearing health practice with valid professional licensing
- Physical retail or clinical location(s) with patient-facing consultation areas
- Staff trained in hearing assessment or vision care (at minimum one licensed audiologist, optometrist, or hearing instrument specialist per location)
- Business insurance covering product liability for dispensed medical-adjacent devices
- Agreement to WHSPR Assist brand guidelines and co-branding requirements (see [Co-Branding Guidelines](../co-branding-guidelines/co-branding-guidelines.md))
- Commitment to recommended fitting protocol and patient follow-up schedule (see [Clinic Deployment Playbook](../clinic-deployment-guide/clinic-deployment-playbook.md))

## 7. Contract Terms

- **Agreement Duration:** 12 months, auto-renewing
- **Volume Commitment:** Quarterly minimums per tier
- **Payment Terms:** Net 30 for Silver and Gold; Net 45 for Platinum
- **Termination:** 90-day written notice; partner must cease use of all co-branded materials within 30 days of termination
- **Territory Exclusivity:** Available for Platinum tier only; subject to separate territory agreement addendum
- **Intellectual Property:** WHSPR Assist retains all IP rights to hardware, firmware, and app platform; partners license branding rights for the duration of the agreement
