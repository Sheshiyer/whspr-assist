## What Is WHSPR Assist Rx?

WHSPR Assist Rx is the first assistive smart glasses system that combines **hearing enhancement**, **camera-based vision assistance**, and **multimodal AI** in a single prescription-ready frame.

It looks like a pair of well-made prescription glasses. Because that is what it is.

Your optometrist fits them with your prescription lenses — the same way they would fit any frame in their office. You put them on in the morning. You wear them all day. They are your glasses.

But inside the temples of these frames, there is something your old glasses never had.

---

## The Problem

Forty-eight million Americans live with hearing difficulty. **Eighty percent of them are not doing anything about it.**

Not because they don't care. Because every option on the table asks them to give up something they are not willing to give up:

- **Traditional hearing aids** cost $3,200 on average per pair. They are visible. Everyone in the room knows you are wearing them.
- **OTC amplifiers** make everything louder — including the noise you are trying to escape.
- **AirPods Pro** block ambient sound, look conspicuous on older adults, and offer nothing for vision.
- **Doing nothing** is the most common choice — and it leads to social withdrawal, family tension, and missed conversations.

Here is the lie that forty-eight million Americans have quietly accepted:

> *"I have to choose between hearing clearly and keeping my dignity."*

That is a false choice. It has always been a false choice.

## The Solution

WHSPR Assist Rx addresses the actual barriers to hearing help adoption:

| Barrier | How WHSPR Assist Responds |
|---------|--------------------------|
| **Stigma** | Smart glasses form factor — nobody knows you are wearing a hearing device |
| **Cost** | $249–$349 retail vs. $3,200+ for hearing aids (90–95% reduction) |
| **Complexity** | No mandatory fitting; calibrated through companion app with optional audiologist support |
| **Single-purpose** | Combines hearing + vision + AI in one frame — replaces multiple devices |
| **Dignity** | Enhancement framing, not medical device positioning |

### How It Works

Two directional microphones — one on each side — use active noise cancellation and echo cancellation to separate the voice in front of you from everything else. The person you are talking to gets clearer. The restaurant noise behind them gets quieter.

Your audiologist calibrates the hearing profile to your specific audiogram. This is not one-size-fits-all amplification. This is **frequency shaping matched to your hearing**.

When you need your eyes to do a little more — when the menu print is too small, when a prescription label needs reading — you tap the frame. The dual-camera system activates. The AI reads the text aloud through the open-ear speakers. Quietly. Just for you.

Nobody at the table hears it. Nobody at the table knows.

## The Ecosystem

### Companion App
- Hearing profile setup with guided audiogram input
- AI assistant settings (text-to-speech voice, language, speed)
- Battery monitoring and charging reminders
- Firmware updates delivered automatically
- Usage insights: hours worn, AI activations, call time

### Provider Portal (for clinicians)
- Upload and manage audiograms for calibrated hearing profiles
- Track hearing trends across visits
- Remote recalibration without requiring an office visit
- Patient engagement dashboard
- Whitelabel branding settings for clinic-branded experience
